Tiancheng Yu (余天呈)

yutc [at] mit [dot] edu  

I am a 3rd-year PhD student in MIT EECS, advised by Prof. Suvrit Sra. Before MIT, I was an undergraduate student in Tsinghua University, major in Electronic Engineering and minor in Statistics. I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Yuan Shen.

In Fall 2016, I visited University of Wisconsin-Madison and worked with Prof. Dimitris Papailiopoulos. In Summer 2017, I was fortunate to join Stanford UGVR program and advised by Prof. Tsachy Weissman.

My research interests lie in the theory of reinforcement learning, especially sample efficiency, robustness and function approximation.
